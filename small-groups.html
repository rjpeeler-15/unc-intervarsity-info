Small Groups at InterVarsity

The What

Small groups are God centered communities where students come together to study God’s word, Worship together, and bear Witness to the Gospel of Jesus.

The Why

Everyone needs a place to connect and belong. Small groups are communities where you can form deep connections and navigate the challenges and the joys of life together.

The How

In InterVarsity we do four year continuing small groups, meaning that the small group you join will stay together throughout your time in college. We want you to build friendships that last four years and beyond. Groups will meet weekly for Bible study but will also spend lots of time together as friends outside of set meeting times.

The When

We have small groups that meet Tuesday, Wednesday, and Thursday nights each week.
